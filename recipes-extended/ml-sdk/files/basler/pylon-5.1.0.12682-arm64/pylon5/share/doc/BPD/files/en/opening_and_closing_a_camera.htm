Opening and Closing a Camera

Opening a camera connection in pylon Viewer allows you to configure a camera. Closing a camera connection makes the camera available to other applications.

Opening a Camera

Before you can configure a camera, you have to open it.

To do so, choose one of the following methods:

  • Double-click the camera in the Devices pane.
  • Right-click the camera name in the Devices pane and choose Open from the context menu.
  • Select the camera in the Devices pane, go to the Camera menu and choose Open.
  • Select the camera in the Devices pane and click the button in the toolbar.
  • Select the camera in the Devices pane and press Ctrl+O.

Closing a Camera

When you have finished using a camera, you can make it available to other applications by closing it.

To do so, choose one of the following methods:

  • Right-click the camera name in the Devices pane and choose Close from the context menu.
  • Select the camera in the Devices pane, go to the Camera menu and choose Close.
  • Select the camera in the Devices pane and click the button in the toolbar.
  • Select the camera in the Devices pane and press Ctrl+L.
