Basler ace GigE

Basler ace GigE cameras offer a Gigabit Ethernet interface and comply with the GigE Vision standard.

Gigabit Ethernet offers the widest technological flexibility with regard to bandwidth, cable length, and multi-camera functionality. It is the right choice for data transfer rates up to 100 Mbit/s and a maximum cable length of 100 meters. It also makes complex setups with several cameras simple.

All Basler ace GigE cameras can use Power over Ethernet (PoE) to provide camera power and data via a single cable. They also offer separate input/output ports for triggered image acquisition or flash control.

The Basler ace GigE camera is available with several resolutions and speeds and with sensors from leading manufacturers.
