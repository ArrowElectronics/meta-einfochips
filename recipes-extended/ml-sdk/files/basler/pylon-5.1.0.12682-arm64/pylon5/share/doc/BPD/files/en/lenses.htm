Lenses

Basler's lens portfolio includes lenses with all common lens mount types, focal lengths, and iris ranges.

Basler offers their own range of lenses, labeled Basler Original Equipment, as well as lenses by other leading manufacturers. While all lenses are tested to ensure full compatibility with Basler cameras, the Basler Original Equipment lenses have been designed specifically for Basler cameras and are manufactured exclusively for Basler.

To find the right lens for your application, visit the Basler website.

Lens Types

  • C-mount lenses
  • CS-mount lenses
  • S-mount lenses

Also included in the portfolio are lens accessories, like mount adapters and spacer rings, which let you securely mount a lens on a camera.
