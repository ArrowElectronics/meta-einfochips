I/O Board

A range of accessories to complement Basler dart cameras is available.

The dart I/O Board and the dart Mounting Cover are at the heart of this accessory group. The benefits of the I/O board include, among others, easier access to the camera's GPIO lines and the ability to work with higher voltage levels.

The benefits of the mounting cover are, among others, that it allows you to cover the open back of the camera and that it provides you with more ways to install the camera.

To view the complete product range, visit the Basler website.
