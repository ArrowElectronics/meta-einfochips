Lens Accessories

Also included in the portfolio are lens accessories, like mount adapters and spacer rings, which are ideal to securely mount a lens on a camera.

Sometimes, your lens may not fit the camera's lens mount perfectly out of the box. If this is the case, choose one of the mount adapters or spacer rings.

To find the right accessory for your lens, visit the Basler website.
