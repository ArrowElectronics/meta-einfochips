S-Mount Lenses

There is a large range of lenses available to cater for different application scenarios and requirements.

Basler's S-mount lenses are available to fit image circles of 1/1.8 to 1/2 ″. They have a fixed aperture with focal lengths of 2.5, 4.0, 6.0 and 8.0 mm and iris ranges of 1.6, 1.8, and 2.0. You focus them by adjusting how far you insert the lens in the mount.

S-mount lenses are especially suitable for Basler's board level cameras with image circles of 1.2 to 1.3 ″.

To find a suitable S-mount lens, visit the Basler website.
