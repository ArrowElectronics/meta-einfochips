Cables

Basler's cable portfolio includes I/O cables, data cables, and power cables of various lengths and with different connectors.

Basler offers their own range of cables, labeled Basler Original Equipment, as well as cables by other manufacturers. While all cables are tested to ensure full compatibility with Basler cameras, the Basler Original Equipment cables have been designed specifically for Basler cameras and are manufactured exclusively for Basler.

To facilitate customizing your cables, Basler also offers open-end cables and a small range of connectors.

To find the right cable for your application, visit the Basler website.
